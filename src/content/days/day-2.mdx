## The Donkeys

While we were stopped, a couple of trailers were backed up and two donkeys (or mules) were being transferred from one to the other.

![Two trailers in a parking lot — the donkey transfer in progress](/images/day-2/IMG_4074.jpeg)

> *The video `IMG_4077.MOV` captured the moment — I was trying to catch the whole transfer but ended up just getting the face of number one. Once the first one was loaded, the second jumped right in to be with its pal.*

You just never know what you'll see along the way. Keeps us alert with the fun things.

I took over driving here — Keith let me drive for **two whole hours**. He pretty much kept his head down looking at his iPad and phone. Think he was nervous? 😉
