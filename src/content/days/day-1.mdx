## The Phone

Uh oh — Keith can't find his phone. I used *Find My* and could see it was somewhere in the truck, but we couldn't hear it ringing. We pulled over. Keith started going through his backpack. I glanced over and saw this:

![Keith's phone — found in a very unexpected location](/images/day-1/IMG_3972.jpeg)

Apparently his 60-pound weight loss disturbed the nerves in his... seat. He didn't even feel it. Back on the road.
